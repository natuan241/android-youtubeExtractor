Extractor Library

This page is about the downloader App if you are interested in the extractor library used by the App refer to the github page.

App

The App basically consists of a dialog window, that is accessible through the "Share" option in your browser or within the YouTube App: YouTube Downloader

Limitations

  • Private videos
  • Live streams
  • Audio is m4a (aac), no mp3 support
